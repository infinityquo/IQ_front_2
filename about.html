CLARION CALL

What is the Clarion Call?

It is the purpose every man is born for, “to add value to him and the world”. THE CLARION CALL as an organization is obligated to help every man and woman in the world to answer this call, through making them aware of such a purpose and how they can answer to it.

About us

The Clarion Call is a division of iQ with a sole mandate of helping people to become great!!! We acknowledge that it is not enough to be just successful. What then is the difference between the two, being great and being successful? Success is embodied in greatness but the reverse is not true. Greatness inculcates personal success and one’s ability to influence those near them and the world towards a successful and meaningful living. Greatness starts within you and ends without you. We are the branch of iQ that will help you to live a significant and great life. In a nutshell, The Clarion Call is a school with the responsibilities of teaching and equipping its students with tools of carving greatness out of their lives
